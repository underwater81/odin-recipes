Cuba Libre

Description

A classic Cuban cocktail of rum, cola, lime and ice. Pour into a tall glass for the ultimate refreshing long cocktail

  • 1/2 lime
  • 50ml rum
  • 100ml cola
  • ice

Steps

  1. Cut 1/2 a lime into four small wedges
  2. Squeeze the juice from two of the wedges into a tall glass
  3. Drop the remainig wedges into the glass and fill with ice
  4. Pour in the rum then fill up with cola
  5. Stir gently
